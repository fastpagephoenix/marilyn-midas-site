Frequently Asked Questions

How do I request a booking?

All private requests begin through the booking request form after unlocking experience details. Each request is reviewed individually.

Is availability guaranteed after submitting a request?

No. Submitting a request does not guarantee availability or confirmation. Bookings are confirmed only after approval and retainer verification.

What is required to secure a booking?

A 50% non-refundable retainer is required to reserve time and availability. Payment instructions are provided only after request approval.

What payment methods are accepted?

Zelle, Venmo, and Apple Pay are accepted. Payment details are provided after approval.

Can I cancel or reschedule?

Requests canceled at least 72 hours in advance may be applied to a future date, subject to availability. Retainers are otherwise non-refundable.

Do you travel?

Yes. Travel availability is limited and released privately. Requests may be submitted based on current and upcoming destinations.

Are professional shoots or collaborations available?

Professional shoots, creative collaborations, and content work may be considered on a case-by-case basis through the booking form.

Do you offer virtual experiences?

Virtual and online availability is limited and varies. Official platforms and access are provided through verified channels only.

What kind of requests are not accepted?

Requests that do not align with discretion, professionalism, and mutual respect will not be considered.
