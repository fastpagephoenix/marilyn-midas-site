The Escapade

Private Rate Card Available Upon Request

The Escapade is for those who desire time that unfolds naturally. More than a brief introduction, this extended experience allows the day or evening to breathe.

Designed for a curated block of time, The Escapade may include dinner, conversation, private hosting, social appearances, or a quiet luxury setting.

Arrangement Notes

Local Phoenix arrangements require the guest to secure the hotel, Airbnb, or private setting and cover personal transportation. If Marilyn is already in your city, the same applies unless alternate arrangements are discussed and approved.

Booking & Retainer Policy

All confirmed bookings require a 50% non-refundable retainer to reserve time and availability. Accepted payment methods include Zelle, CashApp, and Venmo.

Cancellations made at least 72 hours before the scheduled engagement may transfer the retainer toward a future date, subject to availability.

Cancellations made within 72 hours of the scheduled time will result in forfeiture of the retainer unless otherwise agreed upon by Marilyn and the client.

Submitting a booking request does not guarantee availability or confirmation.
