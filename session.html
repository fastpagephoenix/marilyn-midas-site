The Signature Session

Private Rate Card Available Upon Request

The Signature Session is designed for professional shoots, creative collaborations, brand content, private production, and polished visual storytelling.

Ideal for photographers, creators, brands, and approved collaborators seeking a refined visual presence. Sessions may include planning, concept direction, wardrobe coordination, scene preparation, and scheduled shoot time.

Production Notes

Professional shoot requests are reviewed individually. Scope, location, usage rights, deliverables, time, travel, and creative expectations must be discussed before approval.

Booking & Retainer Policy

Confirmed creative sessions may require a non-refundable retainer to reserve time, production availability, and preparation. Accepted payment methods include Zelle, CashApp, and Venmo.

Cancellations made at least 72 hours before the scheduled session may transfer the retainer toward a future approved date, subject to availability.

Cancellations made within 72 hours of the scheduled time may result in forfeiture of the retainer unless otherwise agreed upon by Marilyn and the client.

Creative shoot terms, usage rights, and deliverables must be approved before confirmation.
