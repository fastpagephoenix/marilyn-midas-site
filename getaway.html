The Getaway is a weekend escape crafted for those who want something rare, private, and unforgettable. It is designed for travel, luxury, and uninterrupted time.

Whether the destination is a quiet resort, a city weekend, or a private retreat, The Getaway is built around discretion, elegance, and stepping outside ordinary life.

Travel Requirements

Fly-me-to-you requests require all travel expenses paid, including flight, hotel or lodging, ground transportation, meals, and reasonable related expenses. Travel engagements require approval before confirmation.

Booking & Retainer Policy

All confirmed bookings require a 50% non-refundable retainer to reserve time and availability. Accepted payment methods include Zelle, CashApp, and Venmo.

Cancellations made at least 72 hours before the scheduled engagement may transfer the retainer toward a future date, subject to availability.

Cancellations made within 72 hours of the scheduled time will result in forfeiture of the retainer unless otherwise agreed upon by Marilyn and the client.

Travel, lodging, and related expenses are separate from the listed experience rate unless otherwise agreed in writing.
